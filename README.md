## 🛠️ The Pipeline
I bridge the gap between 3D modeling and in-game physics, specializing in high-performance clothing and character assets for the RAGE engine.

* **Blender:** Precision mesh editing, weight painting, and rigging.
* **GrzyClothTool:** Advanced cloth physics, YMT/YMT generation, and component configuration.
* **OpenIV:** RPF structuring, texture dictionary management (YTD), and final asset testing.

---

## 👔 Expertise
* **Rigging & Skinning:** Ensuring zero-clip movement for MP Male/Female models.
